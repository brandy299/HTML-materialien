Grammar Reference

Reported Speech — How it works

Shift all tenses one step back into the past:

Direct Speech Reported Speech
"We are working on it."She said they were working on it.
"I have finished the report."He said he had finished the report.
"We will send it tomorrow."She said they would send it the next day.
"The order arrived."He said the order had arrived.
"Can you check this?"She asked if he could check it.
💡 Rule: present→past  |  past→past perfect  |  will→would  |  can→could
