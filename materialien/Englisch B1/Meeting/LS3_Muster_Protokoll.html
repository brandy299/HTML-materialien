Meeting Minutes — Office Design Ltd., Düsseldorf
Meeting: Weekly Team Meeting, Düsseldorf Office Date: Tuesday, 6 May 2025  ·  10:00 am Present: P. Webb (Chair), M. Bauer, S. Richter, K. Lange, [Your Name] (Minutes) Apologies: T. Fischer (illness)
1. Matters Arising
P. Webb confirmed that the new furniture catalogue had been sent to all key clients on 28 April.
2. Q2 Sales Update
M. Bauer reported that Q2 sales were up 12% compared to Q1, driven mainly by ergonomic chair sales. The team agreed this was a positive result.

ACTION M. Bauer to prepare a detailed breakdown report by Friday 16 May.
3. New Product Range — June Launch
P. Webb announced that the ActiveDesk standing desk range would launch in June. S. Richter raised a concern about stock availability.

ACTION S. Richter to confirm stock levels with the logistics team by 9 May.
4. AOB & Next Meeting
Next meeting: Tuesday 20 May, 10:00 am.
Minutes to be circulated by P. Webb by end of day.
Element 1
Header — Kopfdaten
Das Protokoll beginnt immer mit den Basisdaten: Datum, Uhrzeit, Ort, Anwesende und Entschuldigte.

Apologies = wer fehlt und warum.
Minutes = wer das Protokoll schreibt (das bist du!).
Present: P. Webb (Chair), M. Bauer,
[Your Name] (Minutes)
Apologies: T. Fischer (illness)
Element 2
Matters Arising
Offene Punkte aus dem letzten Meeting. Wurde das Vorherige erledigt? Kurze Bestätigung genügt.

⚠️ Immer Vergangenheitsform verwenden — nicht "Webb says", sondern "Webb confirmed".
✓ P. Webb confirmed that the
   catalogue had been sent.
Element 3
Agenda Item + Action Point
Was wurde besprochen? Was entschieden? Reported Speech ist Pflicht.

Ein Action Point = eine Aufgabe mit Namen und Deadline. Format:
Name + to + Infinitiv + by + Datum
ACTION: M. Bauer to prepare
the report by Friday 16 May.
Element 4
Reporting Verbs
Nicht immer "said" — nutze präzise Reporting Verbs:

announced → neue Info
reported → Bericht/Zahlen
raised → Bedenken/Kritik
agreed / decided → Entscheidung
confirmed → Bestätigung
P. Webb announced that…
S. Richter raised a concern…
Element 5
AOB & Next Meeting
AOB = Any Other Business (= Sonstiges). Hier kommen Punkte, die nicht auf der Agenda standen.

Das Protokoll endet immer mit dem nächsten Termin und einem Hinweis, wer die Minutes versendet.
Next meeting: Tuesday 20 May,
10:00 am.
Minutes: circulated by P. Webb.
Wichtige Regeln auf einen Blick
1
Immer Vergangenheitsform
said / confirmed / reported — nie present tense
2
Vollständige Namen
"Ms Webb stated" — nie "she said"
3
Reported Speech
will → would · can → could · is → was
4
Action Points
Name + to + Infinitiv + by + Datum
5
Präzise Reporting Verbs
announced · raised · confirmed · agreed
6
Aufbau einhalten
Header → Matters → Items → AOB → Next Meeting
