Learn

📞 Booking a hotel room – on the phone

(Ein Hotelzimmer telefonisch buchen)

I'd like to reserve a room…→ making a reservation
…for three nights, from [date] to [date].→ specifying dates
Could you please confirm the booking?→ asking for confirmation
Would it be possible to have an early check-in?→ polite request
Could you arrange an airport transfer?→ extra service

Sample dialogue

(Beispieldialog)

Hotel:Good morning, The Grand Hotel, how may I help you?
You:Good morning. I'd like to reserve a single room for three nights, from the 14th to the 17th of March, please.
Hotel:Of course. Could I take your name and company?
You:It's Morgan, calling on behalf of Office Design Ltd. Would it be possible to arrange an airport transfer?
Hotel:Certainly. Could you confirm your arrival flight?
You:Flight BA 245, arriving at 4:30 pm. Could you please confirm the booking by email?

❌ Cancelling a booking

(Eine Buchung stornieren)

I'm calling to cancel my reservation.→ stating reason
Will there be a cancellation fee?→ asking about costs
Could I get a refund, please?→ requesting money back
I'd prefer a refund to a voucher.→ stating preference

✉️ Email – confirming arrangements

(E-Mail: Reiseinformationen bestätigen)

I am writing to confirm…→ opening
I have booked a room at…→ arrangements
An airport transfer has been arranged.→ extra services
Could you please confirm…?→ request
Please do not hesitate to contact me if…→ closing
