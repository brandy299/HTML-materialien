Learn

Booking a hotel room – on the phone

When calling a hotel, you need to give specific information and make polite requests.

(Wenn du ein Hotel anrufst, musst du bestimmte Informationen angeben und höfliche Anfragen stellen.)

📞 Key Phrases

I'd like to reserve a room…→ making a reservation
…for three nights, from [date] to [date].→ specifying dates
Could you please confirm the booking?→ asking for confirmation
I have a special request – I would need…→ individual requests
Would it be possible to have an early check-in?→ polite question
Could you arrange an airport transfer?→ additional service

Sample dialogue

Hotel:Good morning, The Grand Hotel, how may I help you?
You:Good morning. I'd like to reserve a single room for three nights, from the 14th to the 17th of March, please.
Hotel:Of course. Could I take your name and company, please?
You:Yes, it's [your name], calling on behalf of Office Design Ltd. I also have a special request – would it be possible to arrange an airport transfer?
Hotel:Certainly, we can arrange that. Could you confirm your arrival flight?
You:Of course. Flight BA 245, arriving at 4:30 pm. Could you please confirm the whole booking by email?

Cancelling or changing a booking

Key Phrases

I'm calling to cancel my reservation.→ stating the reason
I'm afraid I need to change my booking.→ polite apology + change
Will there be a cancellation fee?→ asking about costs
Could I get a refund, please?→ requesting money back
I'd prefer a refund to a voucher.→ stating a preference

Writing an email – travel arrangements

After booking, you often send a follow-up email to confirm arrangements or inform colleagues.

Email Structure

Subject:Travel Arrangements – London Visit, 14–17 March
Opening:I am writing to confirm the travel arrangements for your upcoming visit to our London office.
Details:I have booked a single room at The Grand Hotel for three nights (14–17 March), including breakfast. An airport transfer has also been arranged.
Action:Please find the hotel confirmation attached. Could you confirm your flight details so I can arrange the transfer?
Closing:Please do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.
