Introduction

What is a business offer?

An offer is a seller's formal proposal to supply goods or services at defined terms — a central document in commercial correspondence.

Solicited Offer
Angefordertes Angebot

Sent in response to a customer's enquiry. Must contain all information necessary for the customer to reach a decision.

Unsolicited Offer
Unaufgefordertes Angebot

Sent on the seller's own initiative — "on the off chance that the individual or company may be interested." Must introduce the company convincingly.

"A solicited offer should contain all the information necessary for the potential customer to reach a decision."

Section III

Offer Anatomy

A complete offer in practice. Click any numbered part to see the explanation and model phrases.

From: s.mitchell@officedesign.co.uk
To: j.dickinson@eldarin-stationers.saf
①Subject: Offer for office furniture – your enquiry of 12 May 2025
②Attachment: Catalogue 2025; Price list 2025
③Dear Ms Dickinson
④Many thanks for your enquiry of 12 May 2025. We are pleased to submit the following offer and feel sure we can supply you with all the goods you need.
⑤Office Design Ltd. is a major distributor of office furniture with an excellent reputation throughout the European Union and beyond. Our company manufactures a wide range of high-quality products and already has many satisfied customers throughout Europe and North America.
As regards the goods requested, we are pleased to submit the following quotation and will be pleased to supply you with our ErgoLine range of chairs and desks.
⑥Please refer to the attached catalogue for further information on our entire range. Samples are available on request.
⑦Our price is quoted CIP Frankfurt Airport. Wholesalers are granted a trade discount of 10%. A quantity discount of 5% is granted on orders for 20 or more items.
⑧Delivery will be made within 14 days after receipt of order. Delivery can be made immediately from stock for standard items.
⑨Our terms of payment for an initial order are CWO by bank transfer or letter of credit. Or, with favourable references, 10 days 2%, 30 days net for regular orders.
⑩Please find attached our current catalogue and price list for your information.
⑪We hope our offer is to your liking. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you require further details. We feel sure that a trial order will convince you of the quality of our products and look forward to doing business with you.

Yours sincerely
Sarah MitchellSarah Mitchell (Ms) · Sales Manager · Office Design Ltd.

The commercial backbone of every offer. Be precise about payment, delivery and pricing clauses.

Incoterms® — Delivery Clauses

Incoterms® define who is responsible for transport, insurance and customs. Always state them with a named location. The price includes all costs up to that named point.
IncotermFull nameSeller pays to…Example in offer
FCA [place]Free CarrierNamed carrierOur price is quoted FCA our premises.
CIP [place]Carriage & Insurance Paid ToNamed destination incl. insuranceDelivery will be made CIP Frankfurt Airport.
CIF [port]Cost, Insurance & FreightDestination port incl. insuranceOur price is quoted CIF Lagos.
DDP [place]Delivered Duty PaidBuyer's door incl. customsPrice quoted DDP your premises.
FOB [port]Free on BoardPort of loadingPrices are to be understood FOB Dover.

Discounts

Trade / Cash Discount

Given to wholesalers and trade partners.

Wholesalers are granted a trade/cash discount of 20%.
Quantity Discount

Given for large orders above a threshold.

A quantity discount of 5% is granted on orders for 10 or more items.
Preferential Terms

For regular customers who place ongoing orders.

Regular customers may be granted preferential terms.
Cash Discount (Skonto)

Reward for early payment.

10 days 2%, 30 days net (for regular orders).

Useful Phrases

Standard formulas for each part of the offer. Click "Reveal" to see the polished version.

Reference to Enquiry — Opening Phrases

Many thanks for your enquiry of [date] / the above-mentioned enquiry.
We were delighted to receive your enquiry dated [date] about our [products].
As agreed in our pleasant telephone conversation of this morning, I am pleased to let you have details of our offer in writing.
As regards the goods requested, we are pleased to submit the following quotation and will be pleased to supply you with [quantity + article] at [price] each.

Company Description Phrases

We are a major distributor of [products] with an excellent reputation throughout the European Union and beyond.
Our company manufactures a wide range of high-quality products and already has many satisfied customers throughout Europe and North America.
