The 5 Stages of a Business Call

Every professional phone call follows a clear structure. Tap each stage to see key phrases.

1

Answering the Phone

Opening the call professionally

Company + Name + Offer help

"Office Design Ltd., good morning. This is [name] speaking. How can I help you?"

"[Department], good afternoon. What can I do for you?"

❌ Never say:

"Yes?" / "Hello?" / "What do you want?"

2

Saying Who You Are & Why

Identifying yourself + purpose

Identifying yourself

"This is [name] from [company]."

"It's [first name] calling."

Stating your purpose

"I'd like to speak to [name], please."

"I'm calling about [topic]."

"Could you tell me [question]?"

3

Transferring & Dealing with Problems

Hold, transfer, bad line

Transferring

"Please hold the line."

"Just a moment. I'll put you through."

Person not available

"I'm afraid she's in a meeting right now."

"He's out of the office today. Can I take a message?"

"Would you like to phone back later?"

Bad connection

"I'm sorry, I didn't catch that. This is a bad line."

"Could you repeat/spell that, please?"

4

Leaving & Taking a Message

Note-taking + promises

Leaving a message

"Could you ask her to call me back, please?"

"Please tell him I called."

Taking a message

"I'll pass your message on to her."

"We'll get back to you as soon as possible."

"Could I have your number, please?"

5

Finishing the Call

Polite endings

"Is there anything else I can do for you?"

"Thanks for calling. Have a nice day."

"Nice talking to you. Goodbye."
